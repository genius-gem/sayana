Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sayana Press?

Sayana Press is an injectable contraceptive that prevents pregnancy for up to 3 months.

How often should I take Sayana Press?

One injection is usually required every 3 months.

What if I miss my injection date?

Contact your healthcare provider as soon as possible.

Can Sayana Press stop my monthly flow?

Changes in menstrual bleeding patterns can occur while using Sayana Press.
